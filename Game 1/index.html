  • Player Experience Goals: The experience of realizing I created a fairly boring game.
  • Gameplay: This game is simple just collect the asteroids. You can move your character left and right and jump to reach the platforms. Simply run into an asteroid to pick it up. Right arrrow: move right Left arrow: move left Up arrow: jump
  • Prototype Goal: This prototype is evaluating movement.
  • Features: It combines futuristic chracters with an earth like atmosphere.
  • Setting/Premise: This game is about a robot who must collect asteroids to power a laser cannon. The robot's world is being attacked by evil robots and the laser cannon must be built to destroy the evil robots motherhship.
  • Story: The robot continues to collect different kinds of asteroids in increasingly more dangerous environments until finally the laser cannon is built. Hopefully he has good aim because he only gets one shot. He makes the shot and saves his world.
